Here is another simple model for beginners to make: the one-balloon giraffe.

In this exercise, the pinch-twist is used as a means to solidify a model and keep all its parts firmly in place. It also introduces the concept of offset bubbles, which simply associates two basic skills, making bubbles and making angles, to provide a new variety of shapes that look more subtle and natural.
