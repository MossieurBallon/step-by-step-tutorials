Equipment:

  • 1 light brown modeling balloon for the mane
  • 1 warm yellow modeling balloon for the body
  • 1 marker
  • 1 pump

Not compulsory:

  • 1 white modeling balloon for the eyes

Level:
Intermediate


This photo tutorial is a complementary resource for the video Balloon Twisting From Scratch 22 - The Lion which you can also find on Môssieur Ballon's YouTube channel.

Much like the unicorn figure was based on the horse figure with a couple simple adds-on and a color change, this lion is based on the tiger figure, the main difference being the color and the mane

This is important because as you progress beyond the basics of balloon modeling, you will often re-visit some of your older models and modify them to create new ones. The lion, much like the unicorn, is a prime example of this recycling concept.

(Before starting this tutorial, you may first want to review the tiger tutorial: How to make a balloon tiger)
