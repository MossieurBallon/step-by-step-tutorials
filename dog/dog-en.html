Equipment:

  • 1 modeling balloon
  • 1 pump
  • 1 felt-tip marker

Level:
Very easy


This photo tutorial is a complementary resource for the video Balloon Twisting From Scratch 1 - The Doggy which you can also find on M么ssieur Ballon's YouTube channel.

This little doggy embodies the very basics of balloon sculpture, sort of like the famous "Hello World" of programming. Indeed, it's typically the first balloon sculpture a novice needs to learn to master, because, in doing so, he will become familiar with several important basic notions of balloon sculpture.

To go over these basic skills in depth and enable yourself to put this tutorial in practice in the best of conditions, please feel free to refer to the following lessons:
