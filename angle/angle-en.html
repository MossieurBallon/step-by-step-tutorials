  • 1 pump

This photo tutorial is a companion piece to the video Balloon Twisting From Scratch 9 - Skill 4: Make Angles with Balloons which you can also find on Môssieur Ballon's YouTube channel.

Now, I'm going to teach you how to make angles (right angles, acute angles, or obtuse angles) with a balloon.

This skill is used in the sword tutorial, in the giraffe tutorial, but also in many models we'll learn to make later, namely for making love hearts.
