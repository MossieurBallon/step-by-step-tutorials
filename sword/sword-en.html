A hit with the children, here's another very easy balloon sculpture to make. You will however learn three new and important basic skills: making loops, pinch-twists, and making a sharp angle on a balloon segment.

To go over these basic skills in depth and enable yourself to put this tutorial in practice in the best of conditions, please feel free to refer to the following lessons:
